To add items:
1. Click the button labelled "Add"
   in the toolbar.
2. Choose one of the two item types.
3. Type the quantity you would like
   to add.

To process a transaction:
1. Click the button labelled process.
2. Fill in the required details and click
   confirm.

Note: If a mistake is made, click the restore
button in the toolbar to restore the list to a
previous state
